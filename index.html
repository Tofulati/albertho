About Me

Hello! I'm Albert Ho, a Senior Computer Science undergraduate at UC San Diego, currently pursuing both my B.S. and M.S. in Computer Science.

I’m passionate about the intersection of computer science, bioinformatics, and machine learning. I am particularly interested in designing accelerated solutions for biological data analysis.

My interests lie in leveraging computational models and data-driven approaches to solve complex biological problems efficiently and accurately.

I'm always excited to explore new technologies, deepen my understanding of data-driven science, and collaborate on meaningful projects. Feel free to reach out about internship or research opportunities, I’d love to connect!
